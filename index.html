Dillon Homeschool

Each day will have a major class product that we are working on. You will normally produce homework on that subject which we will review each night. In addition every day you must do the following as a separate activity:

  • Read a book for 30 minutes
  • Write a one-page journal on what you read
  • Identify any words you didn't know. Look them up and write their definition
  • Write at least a paragraph in a personal journal
  • Read Come Follow Me assignment
