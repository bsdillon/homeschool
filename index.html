Can-U-Read Books

These are books we started creating for the purpose of teaching different topics and English in general. They vary quite a lot. Most of the pictures are NOT my own and are used under academic rules for fair use. They are excellent for downloading to a child's device and reading in the car, at bed time and any time you have a spare moment. I try to make these good resources, but you do get what you pay for. :)
