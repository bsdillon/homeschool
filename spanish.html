Spanish Verbs

Remember these rules to make a command verb:

  1. Find the yo form
          hablar -> yo hablo
  2. Take off the -o
          hablo -> habl-
  3. Add in the opposite letter so AR->e, ER/IR->a
         hablAR -> habl- + -e = hable
  4. FOR Él, nosotros, and ellos
         hable, hablemos, and hablen.
  1. FOR NEGATIVE TÚ/VOSOTROS ONLY as in "don't talk"
         no hables no habléis
  1. FOR TÚ/VOSOTROS ONLY just keep the normal letter. In fact vosotros adds a -D
         halba and hablad

Homework

  1. Pick five verbs of each kind (AR, ER, and IR).
  2. Follow the rules to form commands for tú, él, nosotros, vosotros, and ellos.
  3. Repeat for negative form of the command.
