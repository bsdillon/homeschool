Historical Literacy

Each week select 2 or 3 elements from the list below. These are important world history events and they are always relted. Consider if you can linking the three shown below. You must agree as a group on the three items. To determine the link:

  1. Pick a random set of events
  2. Do a LITTLE research about each event
  3. Place them in chronological order
  4. Now they are in order, determine how the first led to the second and to the third
  5. Consider asking a parent or google if you can't find the link.
  6. IF YOU CAN FIND A LINK, write down the link immediately. This is critical to your essay.
  7. IF YOU CANNOT FIND A LINK, go back to the first step.
  8. Write an essay describing the first event, the path to the second, the second event, the path to the third, and the third event. Summarize in the conclusion.
  9. Revize your essay and submit it on Friday.
  10. Be sure to mark out the three items on the list.
