Plan a vacation

Do the following to plan a trip to the country below.:

  • Find it on a map. Take a screenshot that places the country on the world map in a place you can identify.
  • Plan a route to get there. If planes won't do it, find the nearest airport and then estimate a boat or other means to reach the country.
  • Find the exchange rate from US Dollars to the local currency. How much local currency will you get for $100.00 US?
  • Translate "Hello. I am visiting from America" in the local language(s).
  • Describe a landmark in the destination country.
  • Describe a local food you might try.
  • Describe a local event like a famous race or annual festival that you might visit.
