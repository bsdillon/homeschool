Trigonometry book link

As soon as possible, download a copy of the text book on your computer. Each of the 17 chapters is built the same way:

  1. Theory - There is text, diagrams, and lots of practice exercises with full answers. Sometimes there are more than one theory sections
  2. Problems - This is a full set of homework which will show if you learned or not. You may work with your siblings, but you may NOT look at the next section
  3. Answers - We will look at these together, but if you look at the answers yourselves you are just cheating.
