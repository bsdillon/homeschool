Two Trains

    A pair of trains start moving toward each other over 1,100km. Train A is traveling at 55 miles per hour. Train B will cover half the distance in 5 hours.

  1. What is the speed of Train B?
    1. 220 miles per hour
    2. 30 meters per second
    3. 110 miles per hour
    4. 5 miles per minute
  2. How long will it take for the two trains to meet?
    1. 3 hours 42 minutes
    2. 5 hours 33 minutes
    3. 4 hours 27 minutes
    4. 2 hours 30 minutes
  3. At what mile marker will they meet?
    1. 795
    2. 483
    3. 717
    4. 306

    4. Rates

  4. Which of these is not a rate?
    1. I was so sick I burped at least a couple of times per minute.
