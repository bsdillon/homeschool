Practice for the GED

For these test questions you may NOT use the internet or any other tools or books.

  1. Take a screen shot of the question and answers.
  2. Answer each question as best you can.
  3. Describe why you eliminated or chose any of the answers.
  4. Record the actual result.
  5. Look up the real answer and read for at least 10 minutes on the topic. For some subjects this is impossible.

Answer each of these daily questions.
