Complete a new writing assignment each day.
Monday Write as many fact-based sentences as you can in 1 hour.
Then spend 30 minutes putting them into good paragraphs.
Focus on writing groups of sentences with the same topic
that you can organize later. 		Scottish highland games
Tuesday Write as many fact-based sentences as you can in 1 hour.
Then spend 30 minutes putting them into good paragraphs.
Focus on defining good topics and then filling in the
fact sentences. 		Dangers of lead poisoning
Wednesday Write as many fact-based sentences as you can in 1 hour.
Then spend 30 minutes putting them into good paragraphs.
This topic is bound to have an implicit order of events.
Write down things in an order and then form them in a
group of sentences based on the major groups of events. 		Independence of Texas from Mexico
Thursday Write as many fact-based sentences as you can in 1 hour.
Then spend 30 minutes putting them into good paragraphs.
You might consider organizing the sentences based on country
of origin or types of sword. You will need to decide how
to organize the sentences. 		Different types of swords around the world
Friday Write as many fact-based sentences as you can in 1 hour.
Then spend 30 minutes putting them into good paragraphs.
There are so many ways to write about this, but I want
you to focus on describing what the Moai are. Don't worry
as much about the history. 		Easter Island and the Moai.
Saturday Write as many fact-based sentences as you can in 1 hour.
Then spend 30 minutes putting them into good paragraphs.
This is another topic that could be written about from
many perspectives. You will have to choose what to focus
on and that will affect the paper you write. If you write
about history it will be very different than one that
talks about the people, the natural organisms they found,
or the places they visited. 		The Lewis and Clark Expedition.
